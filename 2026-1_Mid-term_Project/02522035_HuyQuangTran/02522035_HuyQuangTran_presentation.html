Problem & Motivation

Why do we need EpistemicScout?

🔍 The Information Explosion
  • Thousands of academic papers are published monthly, making it impossible to synthesize all data to find "white space".
  • The current tools for literature review are not efficient and effective.
  • It is hard to find the "white space" in the literature.
🛑 Bounded Rationality
  • Graduate students and R&D professionals spend months on manual literature reviews and often miss inter-disciplinary connections.
💡 The Agentic Solution
  • We orchestrate a team of LLM agents to automatically cross-reference methods, claims, and results accurately across the corpus.
  • We use Graph to visualize the trending connections between papers.
  • LLM detect the research gaps in each cluster of papers.

Project Specs & Evaluation

Addressing Creativity, Usefulness, Concreteness, and Presentation

✨ Creativity
  • The Dialectical Debate Chamber: Using multi-agent personas (Conceptualist vs. Empiricist) to stress-test research ideas in real-time.
🛠️ Usefulness
  • Solving Bounded Rationality: Alleviates the burden of reading thousands of publications.
  • Finds hidden contradictions across inter-disciplinary boundaries.
🧱 Concreteness
  • Clear pipeline: PDF ➔ NER ➔ Knowledge Graph ➔ Gap Topology Analysis.
  • Actionable metric: 0% hallucination on citations.
🎨 Presentation
  • Interactive Streamlit Web App interface.
  • Hybrid toggle for local privacy (Ollama) vs. cloud speed (Gemini).

System Demo Flow

From uploading a PDF to a formulated research hypothesis

📤 Step 1: Ingestion
  • Upload multiple PDFs through the sidebar.
  • Extract text strictly via PyMuPDF.
🧠 Step 2: Orchestration
  • Orchestrator node triggers Extractor ➔ Synthesizer ➔ Critic.
  • Outputs a structured Knowledge Graph of the research field.
🌐 Step 3: Discovery
  • User explores 'islands' and missing links in the Discovery Map dashboard.
⚖️ Step 4: Verification
  • The hypothesis is thrown into the Debate Chamber.
  • Conceptualist and Empiricist critique the validity.
